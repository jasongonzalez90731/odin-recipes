Fajita Recipe

Home

Description

This fajita marinade recipe results in tender, flavorful fajitas every time. It works well with steak, chicken, pork, or shrimp and can even be used to season bell peppers and onions.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoons salt or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon liquid smoke flavoring
    • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk water, lime juice, olive oil, garlic, soy sauce, salt, and liquid smoke flavoring together in a bowl.
  3. Stir in cayenne and black pepper until combined.
  4. Use to marinate 2 pounds of meat; marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours to overnight before cooking as desired.
  5. Enjoy!
